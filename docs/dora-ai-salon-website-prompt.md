# DORA AI PROMPT — VIENNA GLOW STUDIO

## Main Prompt (Copy-paste to Dora)

Create a premium, editorial-style beauty salon website for "Vienna Glow Studio" — a high-end nail, skincare, and beauty studio located at Mariahilfer Straße 45, 1060 Wien, Austria.

**Brand personality:** Premium, warm, sophisticated, confident. Think "experienced friend who is also a professional." Not trendy or flashy — timeless elegance. The target customer is a woman aged 28-55 who values quality, discretion, and a refined experience. She books online from her phone during lunch break.

**Visual direction:**
- Aesthetic: Editorial magazine meets luxury boutique. Inspired by Aesop, Glossier, and vintage Vogue layouts.
- Color palette: Warm cream (#FAFAF8) as primary background, deep charcoal (#2D2926) for text, muted gold/champagne (#C9A96E) as accent, soft blush (#E8DDD0) for highlights. NO purple gradients, NO generic AI pastels.
- Typography: Serif display font for headlines (something like Canela, Tiempos, or Playfair Display), clean sans-serif for body (Inter or similar). Large, confident headlines. Generous line-height.
- Imagery: Editorial beauty photography — close-up hands with manicured nails, soft focus portraits, minimalist product shots, ambient salon interiors with natural light. NO stock photo clichés, NO fake smiles.
- Layout: Asymmetric, generous whitespace, grid-breaking elements. Use overlap and layering for depth. Mobile-first but desktop should feel like flipping through a premium magazine.
- Motion: Subtle — gentle fade-ins on scroll, smooth hover transitions. No aggressive animations.

**Language:** Primary language is German (Austrian German conventions, Sie-Form). English and Turkish as secondary language options.

**Required sections (in this order):**

1. **Hero section**
   - Large serif headline: "Schönheit, die bleibt."
   - Subheadline: "Nails, Gesichtspflege, Wimpern & Brauen — professionell und mit Liebe zum Detail. Buchen Sie Ihren Wunschtermin bequem online."
   - Primary CTA button: "Jetzt Termin buchen" (gold accent)
   - Secondary CTA: "Leistungen ansehen"
   - Small trust indicators: "Online buchbar ✓  Flexible Termine ✓  Professionelle Behandlungen ✓"
   - Background: Editorial close-up of manicured hands or soft-focus salon interior

2. **Services section** — "Unsere Leistungen"
   - Three category cards: Nails, Gesichtspflege, Wimpern & Brauen
   - Each card: category image, short description, "Mehr erfahren" link
   - Below: list of popular treatments with price from €45-€120
   - Example services:
     - Gel Maniküre (60 min, €45)
     - Nail Art Design (90 min, €65)
     - HydraFacial Classic (45 min, €89)
     - Wimpernverlängerung (120 min, €95)

3. **About section** — "Über uns"
   - Headline: "Seit 2018 Ihr vertrauensvoller Ort für Schönheit in Wien"
   - Short warm text about philosophy, craftsmanship, care for each client
   - Editorial portrait or interior shot on one side, text on the other
   - Small stats: "500+ zufriedene Kunden  ·  6 Jahre Erfahrung  ·  100% Bio-Produkte"

4. **Gallery section** — "Unsere Arbeit"
   - Grid of 6-9 editorial images showcasing work (nails, facials, brow work)
   - Minimal captions
   - Hover effect: subtle zoom

5. **Booking section** — "Termin vereinbaren"
   - Large CTA block with background image
   - Headline: "Ihr Wunschtermin ist nur einen Klick entfernt"
   - Short text about easy booking, reminders, flexible rescheduling
   - Primary button: "Online buchen" (gold)
   - Secondary: WhatsApp button "Per WhatsApp buchen"

6. **Testimonials section** — "Was unsere Kundinnen sagen"
   - 3 testimonial cards with customer first name, short quote, 5 stars
   - Clean, editorial layout — not bubble chat style

7. **Contact & location section** — "Besuchen Sie uns"
   - Address: Mariahilfer Straße 45, 1060 Wien
   - Opening hours: Mo-Fr 9:00-19:00, Do bis 21:00, Sa 10:00-17:00, So geschlossen
   - Phone: +43 1 234 5678
   - Email: hello@viennaglowstudio.at
   - Google Maps embed
   - Social: Instagram link

8. **Footer**
   - Salon name, tagline
   - Quick links: Leistungen, Über uns, Termin, Kontakt
   - Legal: Impressum, Datenschutzerklärung, AGB
   - Copyright: © 2026 Vienna Glow Studio
   - Small line: "Powered by Beauty Booking OS"

**Important constraints:**
- NO purple-to-pink gradients
- NO rounded corner overload
- NO emoji in headlines or body text
- NO generic "We believe in..." marketing speak
- NO stock images of women laughing with salad
- Use real German — not translated English
- Booking button must be visible on every section (sticky header or recurring CTA)

**Technical requirements:**
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Fast loading
- GDPR cookie banner (German)
- Accessible (WCAG AA contrast)
- Smooth scroll between sections
- Sticky navigation header with logo left, menu right, booking button far right

**Final touch:**
Make the site feel like an extension of the salon experience itself — refined, intentional, calm. When a visitor arrives, she should feel she's already being taken care of.
